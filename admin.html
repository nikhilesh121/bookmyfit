How Corporate Employee Management Works
1️⃣
Corporate Signs Up
Company purchases a corporate plan (Pro or Max) for their employees
2️⃣
Employees Get Linked
Admin adds employees to the corporate account via email or bulk upload
3️⃣
Automatic Plan Inheritance
All employees automatically get the same plan as their corporate (Pro or Max)
4️⃣
Corporate Pays, Employees Use
Corporate is billed for all employees. Employees enjoy free access to gyms
